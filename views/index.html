They Should Be Flowers short film and VR companion experience about a six-year-old Black girl who was handcuffed by police in Ontario.

Set in a childlike world of play and wonder, this multi-layered experience uses the latest VR technology to examine Canada’s long standing history of policing Black bodies told through the eyes of a child.
